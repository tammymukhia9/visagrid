The traveler must: - Hold a passport valid for period of intended stay with one blank visa page -Not transit Yanji (YNJ) -Not transiting via Guangzhou or Wuhan -Hold proof of sufficient funds -Hold proof of onward/return flights -Hold all documents required for the next destination -Hold visa for next country where applicable -Confirm with their airline that boarding will be permitted without a visa -Should the traveler not meet the above criteria, a visa may be required.
